Giant TCR maintenance playbook

Keep the Rabobank edition responsive and race ready with a proven routine that protects every contact point.

Precision Longevity Race ready

Routine cleaning

Rinse and wipe down the frame, drivetrain and wheels after hard sessions to prevent grime from embedding in the carbon weave.

Precision lubrication

Apply a quality chain lube, cycle the drivetrain through the gears and remove any excess residue to reduce wear.

Torque verification

Use a calibrated wrench to confirm stem, cockpit, seat mast and crank bolts remain within manufacturer specifications.

Tire pressure discipline

Check PSI before every ride, inspect for cuts or embedded debris and replace tires when the centre tread begins to square off.

Confident braking

Assess pad thickness, ensure calipers track evenly and look for cable or hydraulic wear before it affects modulation.

Drivetrain sync

Monitor the chain, cassette and chainrings for stretch or shark-toothing; service the derailleur for crisp, silent shifts.

Wheel true & hubs

Spin wheels to detect hops, tension spokes as needed and keep hub bearings spinning freely for low rolling resistance.
