Giant TCR Advanced SL ISP Rabobank Edition

The Giant TCR Advanced SL ISP Rabobank Edition is a high-performance racing bike designed for speed and aerodynamics. This bike is a special edition created for the Rabobank cycling team, featuring advanced materials and cutting-edge technology.

Key Features

  • Integrated Seat Post (ISP) for improved stiffness and comfort
  • Advanced SL-grade composite frame
  • Aerodynamic design for reduced drag
  • Lightweight and durable construction
  • High-performance components for optimal performance

Specifications

  • Frame: Advanced SL-grade composite
  • Fork: Advanced SL-grade composite, full-composite OverDrive 2 steerer
  • Seatpost: Integrated Seat Post (ISP)
  • Drivetrain: Campagnolo Chorus 11v carbon (2015)
  • Brakes: Campagnolo Chorus 11v carbon (2015)
  • Wheels: Campagnolo Scirocco 35mm c15
  • Tires: Continental GrandPrix, innerTube, 700x25c

This bike is designed for serious cyclists who demand the best in performance and technology. Whether you're racing or training, the Giant TCR Advanced SL ISP Rabobank Edition will help you achieve your goals.
