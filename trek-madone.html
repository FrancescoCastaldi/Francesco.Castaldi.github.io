Trek Madone SL5 Gen8 - Matte Deep Smoke Black

The Trek Madone SL5 Gen8 is an aerodynamic road bike built for performance and speed. Designed with cutting-edge technology, it delivers lightweight efficiency and a sleek aesthetic, making it a top choice for dedicated cyclists.

Key Features

  • IsoFlow technology for enhanced aerodynamics and comfort
  • 500 Series OCLV Carbon frame for superior stiffness-to-weight ratio
  • Integrated cockpit for a seamless look and improved performance
  • Hidden cables for better aerodynamics
  • Reliable and precise Shimano 105 12-speed groupset

Specifications

  • Frame: 500 Series OCLV Carbon
  • Fork: Full Carbon, tapered steerer
  • Seatpost: Madone Aero Seatpost
  • Drivetrain: Shimano 105 R7100 12-speed
  • Brakes: Shimano 105 hydraulic disc
  • Wheels: Bontrager Aeolus Elite 35 carbon
  • Tires: Bontrager R3 Hard-Case Lite, 700x28c

With its cutting-edge aerodynamics and high-quality components, the Trek Madone SL5 Gen8 is perfect for cyclists who aim for top speeds and a competitive edge.
